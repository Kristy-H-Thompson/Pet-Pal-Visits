About Us

Our Services

SNUGGLE STOP

Our basic visit includes fresh food, water change, a walk and/or playtime, litter box cleaning, and lots of affection and love!
*20-30 minutes in length*

PAWS AND PLAY

Our Paws and Play visit includes everything in our Snuggle Stop, but is extended by thirty minutes so that your pet gets a little extra play time.
*60 minutes in length*

OVERNIGHT STAYS

For over night stays we come into your home to care for your pet(s), and carry out their normal routine that you perform with them daily. They will have companionship throughout the night, and you will have peace of mind!
*60 minutes in length*

WAGS AND WANDER

For dog walks we come to your home and walk your dog(s) on their normal routine walk. We can also pick your dog(s) up and take them to a doggy park of your choice (price may increase by driving distance)

INITIAL CONSULTATION

This complimentary visit is essential for all new clients. We’ll come to your home to spend time with you and your pet(s), allowing you to meet your pet sitter, learn about Pet Pal Visits With Matthew, ask questions, and discuss your pet’s individual needs. The Initial Meet & Greet typically lasts about 30 minutes.
